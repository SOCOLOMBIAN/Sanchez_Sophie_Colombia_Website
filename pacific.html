Pacific Coast

The Pacific Coast is one of Colombia most breathtaking and biodiverse regions. Stretching over 1,300 kilometers, this area is a hidden gem, known for its lush rainforests, pristine beaches, and rich cultural heritage. The region is home to unique wildlife, including humpback whales that migrate here each year, and vibrant communities with a deep African and indigenous influence.

Tourism places in the pacific

Chocó

nuqui

A paradise on Colombia's Pacific coast known for its whale watching, pristine beaches, and untouched rainforest. The perfect destination for nature lovers and those seeking tranquility away from crowded tourist spots.

activities

  • Whale watching (July to October)
  • Surfing in Termales beach
  • Hot springs visits
  • Rainforest hiking

Bahía Solano

The largest town on Chocó's northern Pacific coast, offering a perfect blend of jungle and ocean experiences. Known for its excellent sport fishing and beautiful beaches.

Activities:

  • Sport fishing
  • Scuba diving
  • Visit to El Valle beach
  • Waterfall tours

Capurganá

A hidden gem in the Caribbean region of Chocó, bordered by Panama. This car-free paradise offers crystal clear waters and is surrounded by dense jungle.

Activities:

  • Snorkeling in coral reefs
  • Hiking to La Miel beach
  • Visit to El Cielo waterfall
  • Boat trips to nearby beaches

Valle del Cauca

Cali

The salsa capital of the world, Cali offers vibrant culture, historic architecture, and endless entertainment options. Known for its nightlife, gastronomy, and friendly people.

Activities:

  • Salsa dancing classes
  • Visit to San Antonio neighborhood
  • Tour of Christ Rey statue
  • Explore Cali Zoo

Buenaventura

Colombia's main Pacific port city, offering unique cultural experiences, fresh seafood, and access to nearby natural wonders like marine parks and mangroves.

Activities:

  • Mangrove tours
  • Visit to local markets
  • Seafood gastronomy tours
  • Piangua collection with locals

Lago Calima

One of the largest artificial lakes in Colombia, perfect for water sports and outdoor activities. Surrounded by beautiful mountains and offering perfect wind conditions.

Activities:

  • Windsurfing and kitesurfing
  • Camping by the lake
  • Boat tours
  • Paragliding
